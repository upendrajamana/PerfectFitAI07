About PerfectFitAI

PerfectFitAI is an AI-powered resume enhancement platform designed to help job seekers stand out in a competitive market. Whether you're a fresher or an experienced professional, PerfectFitAI analyzes your resume and provides instant feedback, skill gap analysis, role-based suggestions, and real-time scoring.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by empowering users with tools that make their resume more impactful, ATS-friendly, and personalized.

Resume Analysis
Get instant feedback on your resume's strengths and areas for improvement
Real-time Scoring
Understand how your resume ranks with our comprehensive scoring system
Skill Gap Analysis
Identify missing skills and qualifications for your target roles
